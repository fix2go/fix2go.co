Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What types of payment do you accept?

A: We currently accept cash, Zelle, Cash App, and Venmo. Credit/debit card payments will be available soon.

Q: Do you have a physical store?

A: No — Fix2Go is a fully mobile repair service. We come to your location for repairs, whether it’s your home, office, or other safe space.

Q: What areas do you serve?

A: We currently serve Tampa, Brandon, Riverview, Wesley Chapel, St. Petersburg, Clearwater, and nearby neighborhoods.

Q: What devices do you repair?

A: We repair smartphones, tablets, laptops, and game consoles. Common repairs include screen replacements, battery swaps, and diagnostics.
